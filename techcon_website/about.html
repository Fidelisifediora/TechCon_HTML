History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a premier event attracting thousands of attendees from around the world. The conference has played a pivotal role in highlighting emerging technologies, from AI to quantum computing.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to promote technological innovation, foster knowledge sharing, and provide a platform for networking and collaboration in the global tech community. We aim to inspire new generations of developers, entrepreneurs, and innovators by showcasing cutting-edge advancements.

Notable Past Speakers

Satya Nadella – CEO of Microsoft

Satya Nadella became CEO of Microsoft in 2014, transforming the company with a focus on cloud computing and AI, turning it into one of the world's most valuable tech companies.

Elon Musk – CEO of SpaceX and Tesla

Elon Musk, CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, has revolutionized industries with electric vehicles, space exploration, and renewable energy, driving innovation on a global scale.

Susan Wojcicki – Former CEO of YouTube

Susan Wojcicki helped turn YouTube into the world’s leading video platform, shaping the digital content landscape and revolutionizing digital advertising strategies.

Sundar Pichai – CEO of Google and Alphabet

As CEO of Google and Alphabet, Sundar Pichai has led the company through advancements in AI, cloud computing, and hardware, reinforcing its dominance in the tech industry.

Sheryl Sandberg – Former COO of Facebook

Sheryl Sandberg played a critical role in Facebook's growth, driving its advertising platform and scaling it into one of the largest social media networks in the world.
